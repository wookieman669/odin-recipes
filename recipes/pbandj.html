Peanut Butter and Jelly!

An all time hero and classic, the peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • peanut
  • butter
  • jelly
  • bread
  • love

Instructions:

  1. Grab two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly on one side of the other slice of bread.
  4. Smash em' together!
