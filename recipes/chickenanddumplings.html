Chicken and Dumplings

A warm and comforting dish.

Ingredients:

  • biscuit mix
  • chicken breast (boneless)
  • carrots
  • celery
  • onions
  • garlic
  • chicken stock
  • milk
  • thickener

Instructions:

  1. cook the chicken, put to the side.
  2. make the biscuit mix.
  3. cut the vegetables into bite size pieces
  4. sweat and saute the vegetables in bottom of soup pot
  5. add chicken stock.
  6. bring to boil and add milk, then add in spoonfulls of biscuit dough
